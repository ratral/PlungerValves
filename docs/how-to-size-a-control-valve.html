  • Step 1. Specify the variables required to size the valve.
  • Step 2. Determine the equation constants.

8.1.1 Step 1. Specify the variables required to size the valve.

  1. Water pressure must be managed from 30 bars to 6 bars.
  2. The control valve has a diameter of 300 mm and is smaller than the diameters of the pipe upstream (500 mm) and downstream (400 mm) of the valve.

Service Conditions:

  • Fluid : Water
  • Water Temperature : 20 °C
  • Altitude: 500 msal.
  • \(P_1 =\) 30 bar
  • \(P_2 =\) 6 bar
  • \(q =\) 0.25 LPS
  • Diameter Pipe Upstream \(D_1\): 500 mm
  • Diameter Pipe Downstream \(D_2\): 400 mm

Valve Design (first approx.):

  • Valve: Plunger Valve
  • Diameter Valve \(d\): 300 mm
  • PN-40
  • Control Element : Cylinder SZ-40
  • Control Characteristic: Equal-Percentage Characteristic

8.1.2 Step 2. Determine the equation constants.

  • 100% open
    • Zeta Value \(\zeta_{vs}\): 15.83
    • Flow coefficient: \(K_{vs}\) : 904 \(m^3 / h\)
    • liquid pressure recovery factor \({F_{L}}_{s}\) : 0.775
  • Atmosphere Pressure : 0.9549364 bar
  • Vapor Pressure : 0.0233834 bar
  • Kinematic viscosity : 1.0155278 \((m^2/s)*1e-6\)

8.1.3 Step 3. Determine the piping geometry factor (\(F_P\)).

Figure 8.1: Valve with fittings (Wagner 2008)

according to equation (4.5) :

\[ F_{P} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\frac{\sum{\zeta} \cdot \left(\frac{K_v}{d^2} \right)^2}{0.0016}}} \] Where (4.6)

\[ \sum{\zeta} = (\zeta_{1} + \zeta_{2}) + (\zeta_{B_1} - \zeta_{B_2}) \]

and

Resistance coefficient of the valve in the inlet (4.9): \(\zeta_{1} = 0.5 \cdot \left( 1 - \left( \frac{d}{D_1}\right)^2 \right)^2 =\) 0.2048

Resistance coefficient of the valve in the outlet (4.10): \(\zeta_{2} = \left( 1 - \left( \frac{d}{D_2}\right)^2 \right)^2 =\) 0.1914

Bernoulli pressure number in the valve inlet (4.7): \(\zeta_{B_1} = 1- \left( \frac{d}{D_1} \right)^4 =\) 0.0256

Bernoulli pressure number in the valve outlet (4.8): \(\zeta_{B_2} = 1- \left( \frac{d}{D_2} \right)^4 =\) 0.0039

\(\sum{\zeta} =\) 0.4179

\(F_P =\) 5.216181810^{-6}
